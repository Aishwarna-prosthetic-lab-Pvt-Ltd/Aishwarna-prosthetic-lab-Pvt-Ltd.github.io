about

Aishwarna Mudvari is a prosthetic designer & wax sculptor artist currently based in Kathmandu. Prosthetic makeup is used to create a variety of 3D character looks and effects, from ageing and injuries to all sorts of creatures. Her next Venture is to introduce Wax sculpture for the first time in Nepal. A wax statue is a depiction made using a waxy substance. It is life-like figures of icons and celebrities . she has also worked in movie premgeet 3, Nepal's franchise Tv show Crime patrol Nepal & Zee5 Web Series.
